VGDA is a school club from California State University, Long Beach.

Each semester, VGDA members have the opportunity to work in a studio-like environment where they develop a video game of “shippable” quality. It is through this experience that our members cultivate key real-world job and personal skills, while they also receive a credit on a published title.​

Additionally, VGDA does it’s best to ensure that we remain connected to industry professionals by bringing in speakers, scheduling studio field trips, and other industry related activities.

Come find us on Thursdays from 4 PM to 6 PM in PSY 150 for general meetings and on Fridays from 1 PM to 5 PM in VEC 516A for studio days!
